Billiard

Billiards is a cue sport that is typically played on a rectangular table covered with a green cloth and surrounded by cushions. The objective of the game is to use a cue stick to strike one or more cue balls, causing them to collide with other balls on the table and ultimately pocket them into one of several designated pockets. Billiards is a game of precision, strategy, and skill, requiring players to use angles, spin, and careful planning to score points and outmaneuver their opponents. It is a popular social and competitive game enjoyed by players of all skill levels around the world.
